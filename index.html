Section 1
The Market Opportunity

The global live events industry generates over $1.1 trillion annually (PwC Global E&M Outlook 2024). Yet crowd safety management still relies on spreadsheets, radio communications, and static staffing plans. The human and financial consequences are severe and growing.

Human Cost
287 preventable deaths
Astroworld, Itaewon, Hillsborough, and Love Parade — all due to crowd management and planning failures that predictive AI could have prevented.
Financial Cost
8–15% premium increases per year
Event liability premiums rising annually. Astroworld alone generated 100+ lawsuits claiming over $2B in damages. Astroworld is the floor, not the ceiling.
Operational Cost
$2–4M wasted per major event
Major events spend $2–4M annually on staffing, with 20–30% wasted due to inefficient reactive deployment. AI staffing optimisation recovers this directly.

Sources: Risk Strategies Entertainment Insurance Outlook 2025; PwC Global E&M Outlook 2024; Triple-I Liability Study 2024; DAM Firm Festival Deaths Report 2024.

Section 2
The AICrowdCare Platform

AICrowdCare is not an event app. It is an AI-powered public safety command layer that integrates with every element of event operations — from planning through post-event reporting. It runs billions of simulations against historical data to predict, prevent, and manage crowd incidents in real time.

Module 1
Predictive AI Engine
Runs billions of Monte Carlo simulations against demographic data, event type risk profiles, weather thresholds, venue geometry, and proprietary LEMON deployment data from two Rugby World Cups.
Module 2
Real-Time Command Layer
Live crowd density heat maps with predictive surge alerts. AI-optimised dispatch routing cuts average response time 40–60%. Validated against ICS/NIMS emergency management standards.
Module 3
6-Level Cascading Triage
Proprietary 6-level triage linked directly to AI dispatch. Dynamically reassesses severity as new information arrives. Every action timestamped and geotagged for actuarial audit.
Module 4
AR Wayfinding & Dispatch
Augmented reality navigation for responders and attendees. Compatible with smart glasses and mobile AR. Real-time optimal routing through live crowd conditions.
Module 5
Insurance Documentation
Automated audit-trail generation formatted to Lloyd's of London and Canadian insurance market requirements. Enables premium credits of 5–20% for deploying events.
Phase 3 Vision
Smart City Scalability
The same platform extends to municipal emergency management: protests, New Year's Eve, extreme weather events, or mass casualty incidents — unified city operational intelligence.

Sources: NIST TN 2212 (2023); WHO Mass Gathering Health 2022; AHA Circulation 2020; JAMA Network 2022; Edison Insurance Risk Control 2022.

Revenue Model & Financial Projections

AICrowdCare generates revenue through four independently defensible streams that stack and compound across three commercial phases.

Four Revenue Streams

StreamMechanicsBenchmark & Logic
1. Per-Event License Tiered SaaS fee: $10K (<25K attendees) → $30K (25–50K) → $60K (50–100K) → $100K+ (100K+). Full lifecycle access. Eventbase and Bizzabo price $15K–120K/event. AICC pricing is competitive while delivering measurable ROI. McKinsey 2024: AI safety tech commands 15–30% premium.
2. Intervention Billing $250/AI EMS dispatch · $100/crowd reroute · $15/false alarm filtered · $50/surge pre-alert. 50–500 billable actions per event. JAMA 2022: AI dispatch saves $2,000–4,000 per 10,000 attendees in avoided escalation costs. AICC charges $250 — captures <10% of value created.
3. Insurance Savings Share 15–25% share of verified annual premium reductions. Validated by third-party actuarial review post-deployment season. On a $500K premium, 10% reduction = $50K savings. AICC takes 20% = $10K/year recurring. At 50 clients: $500K/year with zero marginal cost.
4. Annual SaaS Subscription For 10+ events/year: $150K–$500K annual contract. Custom AI training, dedicated account manager, priority deployment. Axon (public safety SaaS) and Tyler Technologies trade at 8–15x revenue. AICC targets $6M ARR by Year 3 = $48–90M implied value at comparables.
